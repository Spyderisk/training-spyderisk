Summary

In this course you will have the chance to learn about and start using the powerful Spyderisk risk assessment tool developed by the team at IT Innovation, part of the School of Electronics and Computer Science at the University of Southampton.

Spyderisk is an Open Source tool that will allow individuals and businesses to conduct a comprehensive cybersecurity risk assessment by modelling the hardware, software, data, people and places in a system and identifying the possible security threats at all parts of that system.Â
